ABOUT US

Hello everyone!

It’s great to have you on the blog and fantastic that you’re taking an interest in who we are and what we do! iCoder is a community blog, which is run and supported by several individuals.

What We Write About

Technology is our core area of writing, however we also post about the environment, computers, the Internet, science, blogging, social media, gadgets and more. A full list of the topics we cover can be found in the footer.

Our Structure

Technology Bloggers is not a business or a company. We are an organisation of people working together to provide a great blog. More information on the people who contribute to the site can be found on the our writers page.

Our Mission

Our aim is to make web development easier and more fun by providing high-quality resources and useful tutorials to our readers. The website has been founded in June 2020. The name “iCoder” came from the founder’s immoderate love for these sweet, friendly and funny animals.

iCoder provides free guides, tutorials, and articles about web development and web design in python. All content on the site is free of charge for our readers.

In addition to articles, we also provide exclusive deals, coupons and discounts on python training for beginner and everything else that can be useful to bloggers, web developers and designers.
